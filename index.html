About us

Born in Mozambique. Built for both sides of the table.

A&D Consulting was founded by a Mozambican professional who has spent years helping businesses in Portugal recruit and integrate Portuguese-speaking workers — and helping those workers build stable lives far from home.

That double perspective is our advantage. We understand what employers need: reliability, clear communication and teams that stay. And we understand what workers need: honest guidance, fair treatment and a real chance to grow.

Today, A&D Consulting is a trusted advisor to companies across Portugal and Germany — building stable, productive, multicultural teams that last.
